Mission Statement

The members of Maine Hindu Temple (MHT) sincerely believe in the teachings of the Hindu philosophy based on Sanathana Dharma (Ageless Wisdom) of the Hindu religion whose tenets are Truth, Love, Peace, Right Conduct, and Non-violence. MHT’s mission is to preserve and promote the Hindu religion, culture, and philosophy.

Objectives

  • Maintain a place of worship for the practice of Hindu Religion
  • Conduct and sponsor religious, spiritual, charitable, educational, social, and cultural activities for the Hindu community
  • Promote understanding and respect for Hindu philosophy
  • Enhance pride in Hindu Religion for present and future generations
