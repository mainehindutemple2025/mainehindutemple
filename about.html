About MHT

Our mission is to preserve and promote Hindu dharma, culture, and values through worship, education, and community service.

Our Story

Maine Hindu Temple began as a community effort to build a home for devotion and culture. We welcome all who seek peace, learning, and service.

Values

Truth · Love · Peace · Right Conduct · Non-violence

Deities

Ganesha, Durga, Shiva, Vishnu, Hanuman, and more. (Update per temple)

Community

Language classes, youth activities, cultural events, and seva.
